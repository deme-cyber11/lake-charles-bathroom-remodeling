About Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling

9+ years of licensed, insured bathroom renovation serving Calcasieu Parish and surrounding communities.

Who We Are

Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling is a locally owned and operated bathroom renovation company serving homeowners throughout Calcasieu Parish and Southwest Louisiana. With more than 9 years of hands-on experience and over 400 completed projects, we have built a reputation for quality craftsmanship, transparent pricing, and reliable timelines.

We are fully licensed and insured in the state of Louisiana. Every project we take on is managed by experienced professionals who understand the unique challenges of remodeling in the Gulf Coast region, from humidity-resistant materials to code compliance with the City of Lake Charles and Calcasieu Parish building departments.

Storm Damage Specialization

Since Hurricane Laura devastated Southwest Louisiana in 2020, we have helped hundreds of homeowners restore and rebuild their bathrooms. Many homes in Calcasieu Parish suffered water intrusion, mold damage, and structural issues that required full tear-out and rebuild. We stepped up during the recovery and have continued to specialize in storm damage bathroom restoration ever since.

Our team documents all work thoroughly for insurance claims, making the process as smooth as possible for homeowners dealing with hurricane and flood damage repairs.
