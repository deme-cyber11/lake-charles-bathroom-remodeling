Bathroom Renovation Experts Serving DeQuincy

DeQuincy is a charming small town in northern Calcasieu Parish with deep roots in Louisiana's railroad heritage, and Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling is proud to bring professional renovation services to this welcoming community. From the historic homes near downtown DeQuincy and the DeQuincy Railroad Museum to the residential streets surrounding DeQuincy High School and the quiet neighborhoods along Highway 12, we provide expert bathroom remodeling tailored to the character and needs of every home.

With more than 9 years of experience and over 400 completed bathroom projects across the region, our licensed and insured team understands what DeQuincy homeowners are looking for. Many homes in this area feature original bathrooms from the mid-20th century with dated tile, worn fixtures, and layouts that no longer meet modern family needs. Whether you want to preserve the charm of your older home while upgrading its functionality or completely transform your bathroom into a contemporary space, our team has the design sense and technical skill to deliver exactly what you envision.

Like communities across Calcasieu Parish, DeQuincy was impacted by the devastating hurricane seasons that struck Southwest Louisiana. Bathrooms are particularly vulnerable to storm damage because of their plumbing connections and moisture exposure. Hidden problems like mold behind shower walls, rotting subfloors, and compromised waterproofing can worsen over time if left untreated. Our storm damage bathroom restoration service addresses these issues head-on with thorough demolition, professional mold remediation, structural repair, and a complete rebuild using humidity-resistant materials suited for the Gulf Coast. We provide detailed documentation for insurance claims throughout the process.

DeQuincy residents trust us for our small-town values and big-project quality. Every job begins with a free in-home estimate where we discuss your needs and deliver transparent, no-surprise pricing. We handle all Calcasieu Parish permits and inspections, and our work meets or exceeds Louisiana building codes. Services include full bathroom remodels, shower conversions, bathtub replacements, tile installation, vanity upgrades, and storm damage restoration.
