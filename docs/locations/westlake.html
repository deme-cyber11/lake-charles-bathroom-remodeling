Quality Bathroom Renovations for Westlake Homeowners

Westlake sits on the west side of the Calcasieu River, just across the I-10 bridge from downtown Lake Charles, and Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling is proud to serve this resilient community. From the quiet residential streets near Westlake High School to the established neighborhoods along Sulphur Avenue and the homes throughout the Westlake city limits, we bring professional bathroom renovation services to every corner of this tight-knit town.

Westlake is anchored by the petrochemical corridor that runs along the river, and the families who work in these facilities deserve bathrooms that are comfortable, functional, and built to last. As a licensed and insured bathroom remodeling contractor with 9+ years of experience and over 400 completed projects across Calcasieu Parish, we understand the importance of quality materials and skilled craftsmanship. Gulf Coast humidity demands moisture-resistant finishes, proper ventilation, and expert waterproofing, and our team delivers on every project.

Like much of Southwest Louisiana, Westlake sustained significant damage during the 2020 hurricane season. Many homes in the area still have bathrooms with compromised subfloors, water-stained walls, or mold growing behind tile and drywall. Our storm damage bathroom restoration service handles the entire process, from tearing out damaged materials to rebuilding your bathroom with upgraded fixtures, modern tile, and proper moisture barriers. We provide detailed documentation for insurance claims and work directly with homeowners to ensure a smooth process.

Whether you need a full bathroom remodel, a tub-to-shower conversion, new tile floors, a vanity upgrade, or bathtub replacement, our Westlake customers receive the same dedication to quality and transparent pricing that has earned us a trusted reputation across the region. Every project begins with a free in-home estimate and includes all permitting and inspections. Call (337) 520-8573 today to get started on your Westlake bathroom renovation.
