Trusted Bathroom Remodeling Throughout Lake Charles

Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling is proud to be the go-to bathroom renovation team for homeowners across the Lake Charles metro area. From the neighborhoods surrounding Prien Lake and the established communities near McNeese State University to the revitalized downtown Lake Charles district and the growing subdivisions along the I-10 corridor, we deliver expert craftsmanship tailored to every home and every budget.

As a licensed and insured contractor with more than 9 years of experience and over 400 completed projects, we understand the unique needs of Southwest Louisiana homeowners. Lake Charles sits at the heart of Calcasieu Parish and has weathered significant hurricane seasons in recent years. Many bathrooms across the city still carry hidden damage from Hurricanes Laura and Delta, including water-stained subfloors, compromised plumbing, and persistent mold behind walls. Our team specializes in storm damage bathroom restoration, handling everything from full tear-outs to insurance documentation so you can rebuild with confidence.

Beyond storm recovery, we offer a full range of bathroom remodeling services designed to increase your home's value and improve daily comfort. Whether you need a complete gut-and-remodel, a tub-to-shower conversion, custom tile installation, or a modern vanity upgrade, our skilled installers bring Gulf Coast craftsmanship to every project. We work with homeowners in Country Club Estates, Prien Lake Estates, University Place, the Morganfield area, and every neighborhood in between.

Every project begins with a free in-home estimate where we walk through your space, discuss your goals, and provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. We manage all permits through the City of Lake Charles and Calcasieu Parish building departments, and our work meets or exceeds Louisiana building codes. From design consultation to final walkthrough, we keep you informed every step of the way. Call us today at (337) 520-8573 to schedule your free consultation and discover why Lake Charles homeowners trust us with their bathroom renovations.
