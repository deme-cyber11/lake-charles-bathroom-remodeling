Professional Bathroom Remodeling for Carlyss Homes

Carlyss is a growing unincorporated community situated south of Sulphur in Calcasieu Parish, and Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling is proud to serve the families who call this area home. From the established residential neighborhoods along Highway 108 and the homes near Carlyss Memorial Park to the newer subdivisions that continue to expand this community, we deliver professional bathroom renovations built to the highest standards of Gulf Coast craftsmanship.

As a licensed and insured bathroom remodeling contractor with over 9 years of experience and more than 400 completed projects, we bring proven expertise to every Carlyss home we work in. This community has seen steady residential growth, with homes spanning several decades of construction. Whether your bathroom features outdated 1970s fixtures that need a complete overhaul or a more recent build that just needs a modern refresh, our team designs and executes renovations that fit your space, your style, and your budget.

Carlyss sits in the path that recent hurricanes carved through Southwest Louisiana, and many homes in the area still carry bathroom damage from those storms. Water intrusion through damaged roofs and siding, flooding from storm surge, and the persistent Gulf Coast humidity create a perfect environment for mold growth, rotting subfloors, and deteriorating drywall behind bathroom walls. Our storm damage bathroom restoration service tackles these problems at their root. We perform complete tear-outs when necessary, treat mold professionally, replace compromised structural elements, and rebuild with moisture-resistant materials that stand up to Louisiana's climate. Every step is documented thoroughly for insurance claims.

Carlyss homeowners appreciate our straightforward approach: free in-home estimates with transparent pricing, reliable project timelines, clean and respectful jobsites, and finished results that exceed expectations. We handle all permits through Calcasieu Parish and ensure every installation meets Louisiana building codes. Our full service lineup includes complete bathroom remodels, walk-in shower conversions, bathtub replacements, custom tile installation, vanity upgrades, and storm damage restoration.
