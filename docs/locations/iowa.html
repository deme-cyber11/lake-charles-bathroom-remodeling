Professional Bathroom Remodeling in Iowa, Louisiana

Iowa (pronounced EYE-oh-way) is a small, welcoming town situated along the I-10 corridor east of Lake Charles, and Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling is proud to bring professional bathroom renovation services to this rural Calcasieu Parish community. From the homes along Old Highway 90 and the neighborhoods near Iowa High School to the residential streets off Highway 101 and the properties throughout the town limits, we treat every Iowa bathroom project with the same care and craftsmanship we bring to every job across the region.

As a licensed and insured contractor with 9+ years of experience and more than 400 completed bathroom remodels, we understand the specific needs of Iowa homeowners. Many homes in this community were built decades ago and feature original bathrooms that are overdue for an update. Outdated tile, worn fixtures, inefficient layouts, and aging plumbing are common issues we address every day. Whether you want a full bathroom gut-and-remodel to modernize your space or a targeted upgrade like a new walk-in shower, updated vanity, or fresh tile floors, our skilled team delivers results that last.

Iowa was also affected by the hurricanes that swept through Southwest Louisiana, and storm damage to bathrooms remains a concern for many homeowners. Water intrusion from roof damage, flooding, or broken windows can cause lasting problems including rotting subfloors, warped cabinetry, and mold growth behind walls. Our storm damage bathroom restoration service is comprehensive, covering full tear-out, mold treatment, structural repair, and complete rebuild with modern, moisture-resistant materials. We provide thorough documentation for insurance claims to make the process as smooth as possible.

Iowa families appreciate our honest estimates, on-time project completion, and respect for their homes. We handle all permits required by Calcasieu Parish and ensure every installation meets Louisiana building codes. Our services include full bathroom remodels, shower conversions, bathtub replacements, tile installation, vanity upgrades, and storm damage restoration.
