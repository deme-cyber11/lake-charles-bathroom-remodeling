Southwest Louisiana has faced significant storm damage in recent years, and Jennings was no exception. Hurricanes and tropical storms bring high winds, heavy rain, and flooding that can severely damage bathrooms through water intrusion, roof leaks, and rising water levels. These events leave behind rotting subfloors, warped vanities, compromised plumbing connections, and hidden mold that grows unchecked behind tile and drywall. Our storm damage bathroom restoration service is built for exactly these situations.
