Bathroom Vanity Upgrade Services

Your bathroom vanity is one of the most used and most visible features in the room. An outdated or damaged vanity can make the entire bathroom feel tired, while a well-chosen upgrade instantly refreshes the space. At Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling, we install single vanities, double vanities, and custom vanity configurations for homeowners throughout Lake Charles and Calcasieu Parish. With 9+ years of experience and 400+ completed projects, our licensed and insured team ensures a flawless installation every time.

Single vs. Double Vanity

Choosing between a single and double vanity depends on your bathroom size, layout, and how many people use the space. A single vanity works well in guest bathrooms, powder rooms, and smaller hall bathrooms where space is limited. A double vanity is the preferred choice for master bathrooms, giving two people their own sink, mirror, and countertop space. We help you measure your bathroom and select the right configuration so the vanity fits perfectly and the plumbing lines up correctly.

Countertop Materials

The countertop is the most visible part of your vanity, and the material you choose sets the tone for the entire bathroom. We install countertops in granite, quartz, marble, solid surface, and laminate. Granite and quartz are the most popular choices in the Lake Charles area because they resist moisture, stains, and daily wear. Marble provides an upscale, timeless look, while solid surface and laminate offer budget-friendly options that still look polished. Each countertop is custom-measured and cut with precision sink openings.

Sinks and Faucets

We offer a full range of sink styles to complement your new vanity, including undermount sinks for a clean, seamless countertop edge, drop-in sinks for easy installation, and vessel sinks that sit on top of the counter as a design statement. Pair your sink with a modern faucet in brushed nickel, matte black, chrome, or oil-rubbed bronze. We handle all plumbing connections and ensure everything drains properly and operates without leaks.

Mirrors and Lighting

A vanity upgrade is not complete without the right mirror and lighting. We install framed mirrors, frameless mirrors, and medicine cabinets that provide both style and storage. For lighting, we recommend LED vanity lights or sconces mounted at the right height to eliminate shadows and provide even illumination for grooming. Our team coordinates the mirror size and light placement with your vanity dimensions so everything looks proportional and balanced.

Storage Solutions

Bathroom storage is a challenge in many Lake Charles homes, especially in older properties throughout Calcasieu Parish. A new vanity with soft-close drawers, pull-out organizers, and interior shelving keeps your bathroom tidy and functional. We also install floating vanities that create a modern, open feel while making floor cleaning easier. For larger bathrooms, we can add linen towers or side cabinets that match your vanity for a cohesive look.
