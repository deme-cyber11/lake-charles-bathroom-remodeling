Hurricane and Flood Damage Repair

Hurricane-force winds can rip off roofing and allow rain to saturate interior walls. Flooding pushes contaminated water through floors, baseboards, and cabinetry. Both scenarios cause serious damage to bathrooms, destroying drywall, subfloor, insulation, vanities, and fixtures. Our storm damage restoration process begins with a thorough assessment of the affected bathroom to determine the full extent of the damage. We then develop a detailed scope of work that covers every repair needed to bring your bathroom back to pre-storm condition or better.

Mold Remediation

Mold growth is one of the most dangerous consequences of water damage in Southwest Louisiana. Bathrooms are especially vulnerable because they already contain moisture, and storm-related water intrusion creates the perfect conditions for mold to spread quickly behind walls, under floors, and around plumbing fixtures. Our team identifies and removes all mold-affected materials, treats structural components with antimicrobial solutions, and installs moisture barriers to prevent future growth. We follow industry-standard remediation protocols to ensure your rebuilt bathroom is safe and healthy.

Complete Tear-Out and Rebuild

In many storm-damaged bathrooms, a partial repair is not enough. Water-saturated drywall, warped subfloor, and compromised framing need to be torn out completely before rebuilding can begin. We handle the full demolition process, haul away debris, and rebuild your bathroom from the studs out. This includes new moisture-resistant drywall, updated plumbing and electrical, fresh tile work, a new vanity and fixtures, and a complete finish-out. The result is a bathroom that looks and functions like new.

Insurance Claims Documentation

Dealing with insurance companies after a hurricane can be stressful and time-consuming. We make the process easier by providing thorough documentation at every stage of your bathroom restoration. This includes detailed before-and-after photographs, itemized scopes of work with material and labor breakdowns, and written progress reports. Our documentation has helped many Calcasieu Parish homeowners receive fair settlements from their insurance providers. While we do not file claims on your behalf, we give you everything you need to support your case.
