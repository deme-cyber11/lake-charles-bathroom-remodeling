Soaking Tubs and Freestanding Designs

Modern soaking tubs are built for comfort. They sit deeper than standard tubs, allowing you to fully immerse and unwind after a long day. We install drop-in soaking tubs that fit within an existing alcove as well as freestanding tubs that serve as a dramatic focal point for larger bathrooms. Freestanding tubs are available in acrylic, cast iron, and composite stone finishes, and we help you select the style, size, and material that works best for your space and budget.

Walk-In Tubs for Accessibility

Walk-in tubs are an excellent solution for seniors and homeowners with limited mobility throughout the Lake Charles area. These tubs feature a low-threshold door, built-in seating, grab bars, and anti-slip flooring that make bathing safer and more comfortable. Many walk-in models also offer therapeutic features like hydrotherapy jets and heated surfaces. Our team ensures proper installation with watertight seals and correct plumbing connections, so your walk-in tub performs reliably for years.

Plumbing Considerations

Replacing a bathtub is not just about swapping one unit for another. The plumbing behind the walls and under the floor must be evaluated and often updated. Drain lines, supply lines, and overflow assemblies may need to be repositioned to fit your new tub. If your home experienced storm damage or has older galvanized piping common in many Lake Charles properties, we can upgrade your plumbing as part of the replacement project. All work meets Louisiana plumbing codes, and we handle permits through Calcasieu Parish when required.

Surround and Finish Options

A new bathtub deserves a fresh surround to match. We install tile surrounds in ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone, as well as solid-surface acrylic panels for a low-maintenance option. Add a new faucet set, updated hardware, and coordinated lighting to complete the transformation. Our team ensures every detail is finished to a high standard, from the caulk lines to the trim work.
