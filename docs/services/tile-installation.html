Tile Types We Install

We work with the full range of bathroom tile materials to match your style and budget. Ceramic tile is a versatile, cost-effective option that comes in thousands of colors and patterns. Porcelain tile offers superior density and water resistance, making it ideal for shower walls and bathroom floors in the humid Lake Charles climate. Natural stone tiles like marble, travertine, and slate provide a premium look with unique veining and texture. We also install popular styles like subway tile, large-format tiles, hexagonal mosaics, and decorative accent borders.

Shower Tile Installation

The shower is where tile installation matters most. Improper installation leads to water damage, mold growth, and costly repairs, especially in Southwest Louisiana where humidity levels are high year-round. Our shower tile process starts with proper substrate preparation, followed by a waterproofing membrane system that protects your walls and framing. We then set tile with the right thinset for wet environments, apply grout, and seal everything to create a watertight enclosure that looks great and lasts for decades.

Floor Tile Installation

Bathroom floor tile must be both attractive and safe. We install slip-resistant floor tile options that meet safety standards while still looking sharp. Our team prepares the subfloor properly, ensuring it is level and stable before laying tile. Whether you prefer the warmth of wood-look porcelain planks, the elegance of marble, or the classic look of penny tile, we install floor tile that handles moisture, foot traffic, and the daily demands of a working bathroom.

Backsplash and Accent Tile

A well-placed backsplash or accent wall can elevate your bathroom from ordinary to exceptional. We install tile backsplashes behind vanities and sinks, as well as feature walls that serve as a design focal point. Popular choices include hand-painted decorative tile, glass mosaic strips, and natural stone ledger panels. These details add visual interest and personality to your bathroom without requiring a full remodel.

Waterproofing and Moisture Protection

Living in Calcasieu Parish means dealing with high humidity and the threat of storm-driven water intrusion. Every tile installation we perform includes proper waterproofing behind the tile, sealed grout joints, and moisture barriers at critical transitions. This protects the structure of your home and prevents the mold and mildew problems that are common in bathrooms across the Gulf Coast region.
