Professional Shower Remodeling Services

Your shower is the centerpiece of your daily routine, and it should be a space that feels modern, comfortable, and built to last. At Lake Charles Bathroom Remodeling, we deliver expert shower remodeling services for homeowners throughout Lake Charles and Calcasieu Parish. Whether you want a sleek walk-in shower, a tub-to-shower conversion, or a complete custom tile upgrade, our licensed and insured team brings over 9 years of experience and 400+ completed projects to every job.

Walk-In Shower Installation

Walk-in showers are one of the most popular upgrades we install across Calcasieu Parish. They offer a clean, open look that makes any bathroom feel larger and more contemporary. We build walk-in showers with custom tile, frameless or semi-frameless glass enclosures, linear drains, and built-in niches for storage. Every walk-in shower we install is properly waterproofed and sloped to prevent water damage, which is especially important in the humid Lake Charles climate.

Tub-to-Shower Conversions

Many homeowners in Lake Charles are choosing to remove an underused bathtub and replace it with a spacious walk-in shower. A tub-to-shower conversion reclaims valuable floor space, improves accessibility, and gives your bathroom a modern refresh. Our team handles the complete conversion process including tub removal, plumbing reconfiguration, new shower pan installation, tile work, and glass enclosure fitting. We manage all necessary permits through the City of Lake Charles.

Custom Tile and Glass Enclosures

The tile and glass you choose define the look and feel of your new shower. We install a wide range of tile options including large-format porcelain, classic subway tile, natural stone, and decorative mosaic accents. Pair your tile with a frameless glass enclosure for a seamless, spa-like appearance. Our skilled tile installers ensure every cut is precise and every grout line is clean, delivering results that look professional and hold up for years.

Accessibility and ADA-Friendly Options

For homeowners who need improved accessibility, we offer barrier-free shower designs with zero-threshold entries, grab bars, built-in bench seating, and handheld showerheads. These features make showering safer and more comfortable for seniors and anyone with mobility challenges. Our accessible shower installations meet ADA guidelines and are popular throughout the Lake Charles area, especially after storm damage forces a bathroom rebuild.

Built for the Gulf Coast

Lake Charles weather demands waterproofing done right. Every shower we build uses industry-leading moisture barriers and waterproofing systems to protect your walls and subfloor from humidity and potential storm-related water intrusion.
