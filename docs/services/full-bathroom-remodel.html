What a Full Remodel Includes

A full bathroom remodel means we strip the room down to the studs and rebuild it from the ground up. This includes demolition and disposal, rough plumbing and electrical work, new subflooring and moisture barriers, custom tile installation for walls and floors, fixture upgrades, vanity and countertop installation, modern lighting design, and a complete finish-out with paint and trim. Every project is tailored to your style, budget, and the specific needs of your Lake Charles home.

Custom Tile, Fixtures, and Finishes

We work with a wide range of materials to bring your vision to life. Choose from ceramic, porcelain, or natural stone tile for your shower surround and flooring. Select from modern vanity styles with granite, quartz, or marble countertops. Upgrade your fixtures with water-efficient faucets, rain showerheads, and contemporary lighting packages. Our design team helps you coordinate finishes so everything works together seamlessly.

Permits and Code Compliance

Full bathroom remodels in Lake Charles and Calcasieu Parish often require building permits, especially when plumbing or electrical lines are moved. We handle all permit applications and inspections through the City of Lake Charles and Calcasieu Parish building departments. Our licensed contractors ensure every project meets current Louisiana building codes, giving you peace of mind that the work is done right.

Storm Damage Rebuilds

Living along the Gulf Coast means dealing with hurricanes and tropical storms. Many of our full remodel projects start as storm damage repairs. Whether your bathroom suffered water intrusion, mold damage, or structural issues from a hurricane, we have the experience to tear out what cannot be saved and rebuild it better than before. We also provide detailed documentation to support your insurance claim.
